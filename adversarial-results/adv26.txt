# Synthesis: Is a Tomato a Fruit?

## The Answer

A tomato is botanically a fruit (specifically a berry). It is culinarily and legally classified as a vegetable. These classifications operate in different ontological systems and are not contradictory. **Both are simultaneously correct within their respective domains.**

The epistemically honest answer requires specifying the classification system. Without that specification, the question is arguably malformed — "vegetable" is not a botanical category, so the question itself mixes taxonomic frameworks.
