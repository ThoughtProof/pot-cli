## What "Speed of Gravity" Actually Means

This question is subtly ambiguous. What's been measured is the speed of **gravitational radiation** (waves). The static gravitational field doesn't "propagate" in the conventional sense â€” it already exists. Only **changes** in the gravitational field propagate at c. A historically important confusion (raised by Van Flandern, 2002) is that planetary orbits seem to "point" at where the Sun *is*, not where it *was* â€” suggesting instantaneous gravity.
