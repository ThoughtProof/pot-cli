# Goldfish Memory: Synthesis of Multi-Agent Analysis

## Core Answer

**The 3-second goldfish memory claim is a debunked myth.** Goldfish possess functional memory lasting days to months, navigate their environments through spatial mapping and associative learning, and demonstrate cognitive capabilities consistent with other teleost vertebrates. All four proposals reach this conclusion, and it is well-supported by the scientific literature.

Goldfish navigate their tanks through:
1. **Spatial mapping** — building cognitive maps using visual landmarks (decorations, filters, tank walls)
2. **Associative learning** — linking environmental cues to outcomes (owner approaching → food)
3. **Habituation** — reducing fear responses to familiar, non-threatening stimuli over repeated exposure
4. **Temporal conditioning** — anticipating feeding times via internal circadian mechanisms

These are the same broad mechanisms used by most vertebrates, operating at a simpler scale.
