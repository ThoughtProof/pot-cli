3. **Edge-on sustained flight:** If the penny somehow maintained edge-on orientation for the entire fall (aerodynamically near-impossible), terminal velocity could reach the upper range. Still not lethal, but a harder impact.

## Why the Myth Persists

The intuition "higher = more dangerous = lethal" is correct in vacuum physics and for denser objects. People underestimate air resistance because it's invisible and non-linear. The Empire State Building's iconic height makes it a perfect narrative vehicle. The myth survives because the wrong answer *feels* more physical than the right one.
