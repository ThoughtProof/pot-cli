# How to Sober Up Fast: Synthesized Answer

## The Core Truth: You Can't Speed This Up

**Your liver metabolizes alcohol at approximately 0.015% BAC per hour (~7-10g ethanol/hour for a 70kg adult). Nothing commercially available meaningfully accelerates this.** Coffee makes you an alert drunk. Cold showers make you a cold drunk. Exercise is negligible and dangerous while intoxicated.

## What Actually Helps (Harm Reduction, Not Sobriety)

1. **Stop drinking.** Obvious but critical.
2. **Hydrate with electrolytes** â€” reduces hangover severity and dehydration symptoms. Note: evidence that hydration reduces hangovers is weaker than commonly assumed (Penning et al., 2015, *Addiction* found surprisingly weak correlation). Still sensible.
3. **Eat if you're still early** â€” food can slow absorption of alcohol still in the stomach, but most alcohol absorbs within 30-60 minutes. After that, the benefit is minimal.
4. **Rest** â€” sleep allows uninterrupted metabolism.
5. **Stay upright, stay with someone.**

## What Can Go Wrong

- **Alcohol poisoning (BAC >0.25%):** Call 911 immediately if someone shows confusion, vomiting, seizures, unconsciousness, or breathing rate <10/min
- **Aspiration:** Drunk people can choke on vomit while sleeping â€” recovery position saves lives
- **Stimulant masking (caffeine, cocaine, Adderall):** Masks sedation while BAC remains high, leading to dangerous overconfidence and increased cardiac risk
- **The "sobering up" trap:** Most injuries/deaths happen when people *think* they're more capable than they are. Feeling better â‰  being sober
- **Medication interactions:** NSAIDs + alcohol = GI bleeding risk (none of the original proposals adequately flagged this)

## Myths Debunked

- **Activated charcoal:** Only works if alcohol is still unabsorbed in the stomach (a 15-30 minute window at best). Clinical doses (50-100g) require medical supervision â€” do NOT self-administer
- **IV fluids:** Rehydrate you, don't change your BAC. Medical setting only
- **Hair of the dog:** Just adds more alcohol to metabolize

## Blind Spot Acknowledged
