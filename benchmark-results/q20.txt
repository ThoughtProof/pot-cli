# Synthesized Answer: What Clothing Is Illegal to Wear While Driving in the UK?

## The Correct Answer

**No specific clothing item is automatically illegal to wear while driving in the UK.** No UK statute bans any named garment, footwear, or accessory outright. The legal framework is entirely conditional:

- **Highway Code Rule 97** advises (using "should," NOT "MUST") that drivers "should ensure that clothing and footwear do not prevent you using the controls in the correct manner."
- **Road Traffic Act 1988, s.3** prohibits careless/inconsiderate driving ‚Äî clothing becomes relevant only as *evidence* of impaired control.
- **Construction & Use Regulations 1986, Reg 104** requires proper control of the vehicle.

The critical legal distinction: wearing flip-flops, padded gloves, high heels, or even a motorcycle helmet while driving a car is not *per se* illegal. It becomes a legal issue **only if it actually impairs your ability to control the vehicle.**
